About Us

Driving Global Change

International Global Affairs Council

The International Global Affairs Council (IGAC), founded on August 14, 2023, is a youth-led organization dedicated to empowering the next generation of leaders through diplomacy, entrepreneurship, and essential skills development. By fostering critical thinking, leadership, negotiation, and cross-cultural communication, IGAC equips young people to tackle global challenges and drive meaningful change. With a strong focus on collaboration, teamwork, and resilience, IGAC provides a platform for youth to engage in global discussions, overcome obstacles, and shape the future as confident change-makers.

What We Do

At IGAC, we embrace collaboration, teamwork, and resilience as the driving forces behind innovation and empowerment. Our goal is to equip young people with the skills and opportunities needed to overcome challenges and lead with confidence on a global stage. Our mission is to promote youth-driven diplomacy by empowering emerging leaders to engage in meaningful discussions and address global issues directly. At the International Global Affairs Council, we prioritize collaboration, dedication, and perseverance, fostering a mindset that enables young individuals to overcome obstacles and achieve success.

Diplomacy & Leadership

Empowering youth through Model United Nations (MUN) conferences and fostering leadership skills.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Organizing business summits to inspire creativity and strategic thinking.

Global Collaboration

Encouraging cross-cultural communication and diversity of thought.

Non profit

Working non-profit to encourage youth leadership.
